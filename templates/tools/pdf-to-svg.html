Best fit

Use SVG when you need the drawing, not a rewritten document.

This export is designed for sheets where linework, orientation, symbols, title blocks, and text placement matter. It is a better fidelity target than Word for architectural, structural, and engineering PDFs.

Tips

  • Editable text is easier when text stays as text instead of paths.
  • Text as path is safer when fonts are missing or appearance must match exactly.
  • SVG preserves page geometry much better than DOCX for drawings with rotated sheets and dense linework.
  • All pages exports one SVG per PDF page and bundles them into a ZIP when needed.
  • Pair small pages combines front/back business-card style pages into one wider SVG while leaving extra pages separate.
