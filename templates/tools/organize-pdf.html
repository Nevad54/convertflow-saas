Tips

  • Drag a page thumbnail and drop it between other pages to reorder.
  • Page labels below each thumbnail always reflect the current position.
  • You can also duplicate a page — just note this tool reorders existing pages only.
  • Combine with Remove Pages to delete unwanted pages first.
  • Works on PDFs of any size — thumbnails are generated at 72 DPI for speed.
