Tips

  • Cropping uses the PDF cropbox — the underlying content is preserved, just hidden. Some viewers may still show the original page size.
  • A4 pages are 210 × 297 mm. Typical scanners add 5–15 mm of border around the content.
  • Start with equal values on all sides (e.g. 10 mm) to remove symmetric borders.
  • If the margin total exceeds the page size, the crop will be rejected with an error.
