Tips

  • Use Whiteout first to cover original content, then place replacement text on top.
  • Use Signature to draw, type, or upload your signature — then click the page to place it.
  • Use Stamp to mark documents DRAFT, APPROVED, CONFIDENTIAL, and more.
  • Use Highlight, Underline, or Strikethrough to annotate passages.
  • Use Bring to Front / Send to Back in the inspector to layer overlapping elements.
  • This editor overlays new content — it does not rewrite original embedded PDF text or vector shapes.
