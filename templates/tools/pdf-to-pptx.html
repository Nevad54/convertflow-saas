Tips

  • fidelity preserves each PDF page as a slide background and overlays detected text in editable boxes.
  • auto-pair small pages is useful for business cards and other front/back mini layouts that should share a paired canvas.
  • auto tries fidelity first, then falls back to AI or basic reconstruction if needed.
  • ollama, github, and openai rebuild editable title-and-bullet slides from the page content.
  • basic is the fastest option but loses most layout and visual styling.
