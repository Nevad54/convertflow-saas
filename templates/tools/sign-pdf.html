How it works

Sign without printing or scanning.

Tips

  • Use a mouse, stylus, or touchscreen for best signature quality.
  • The signature background is transparent — it sits cleanly on top of the PDF content.
  • Choose bottom right for most forms, or center for signature pages.
  • The signature is embedded at your chosen page. To sign multiple pages, apply this tool again.
  • Use a larger width (80–110 mm) for A4 pages, smaller for compact forms.
