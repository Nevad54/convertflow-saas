Tips

  • fidelity preserves the original PDF page visually inside Word. On standard documents it can add detected text overlays where that is reliable.
  • small-format Word exports like business cards now stay visual-first so the DOCX opens more reliably in Word.
  • auto-pair small pages is useful for business cards and other front/back mini layouts that should appear side by side.
  • auto tries fidelity first, then falls back to AI or basic reconstruction if needed.
  • ollama rebuilds editable text structure from page images and is better when editability matters more than exact layout.
  • basic is fast but loses most formatting and visual elements.
